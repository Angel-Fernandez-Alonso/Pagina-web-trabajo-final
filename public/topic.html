The Metaverse

What is the Metaverse?

The Metaverse is a collective 3D virtual space created by the usage of virtual reality and other technologies. This results in a network of digital words where people can enter and interact with each other. It gains value using technologies such as virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR), blockchain and cryptocurrency, AI, and tokens.

What is it used for?

The metaverse can be used for entertainment, education, work, social interaction and even business. You can play videogames with friends when you are not fisically with them, attend virtual concerts, visit art galleries, or even go to virtual classrooms. It can also be used for companies that wants to create virtual offices where employees can work and have meetings. Thanks to the metaverse, people can interact with people from all over the world.

Related Technologies

  • Virtual Reality (VR). Used for people to experience immersive digital environments, like if they were physically there.
  • Augmented Reality (AR). Used to overlay digital information onto the real world.
  • Artificial Intelligence. Used as assistant in the metaverse, where you can ask questions and get help.
  • Blockchain and NFTs. Used to create digital assets and gain real money with them.
